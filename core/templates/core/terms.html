Quiztainment (the "Competition") is open to all Pakistani nationals aged 18 and above, except management, promoter, staff, their immediate families, and anyone professionally associated with the competition.

ENTER THE COMPETITION:

Register yourself with QUIZTAINMENT, set up a username and password and then log in to pay and play the quiz.

REGISTERATION:

Registration is a prerequisite for entering into competition. You need to fill out the fields in the registration form with all the necessary details and submit the same. Only after registration, you will be able to log in. Use the username and the password as set you while registering yourself with Quiztainment to log in.

PAYMENT:

Once you are logged in, you will be directed to the dashboard where you need to click play quiz which will take you to the payment page for making payment of the entry fee. Fill out the necessary details after choosing your preferred payment mode and make payment of your entry fee.

THE QUIZ COMPETITION:

Upon successful registration and payment of the entry fee, you will become eligible for QUIZTAINMENT. Please note that you cannot cancel or pause the competition once you begin the quiz. Before entering the competition, make sure you have a reliable internet connection. You cannot close or refresh the browser, nor you can click back. You will get twenty (20) minutes to answer twenty-five (25) multiple-choice questions (“Quiz”). You may check your performance on the dashboard for real-time results/scores. There is no restriction on the number of attempts. Once registered you can play the quiz as many times as you like by paying the fee for each entry. Entries made during the entry opening date and the closing date shall be considered only. HOW TO WIN:

In order to qualify a contestant is required to get a minimum of 60% of answers correct. Contestants scoring the highest percentage/marks in lesser time will be declared the winner. In case there is more than one winner in any prize category, the prize value will be equally distributed among the winners. A contestant winning any one of the prizes will not be considered for any other prize at the end of the competition as a contestant is eligible for one prize only. For multiple entries by a contestant, only the best score and time shall be considered.
The quiz competition may be cancelled, or the date of entries may be extended, as deemed fit by the management if requisite entries are not received by the closing date, and no prize will be awarded to any entrant.
